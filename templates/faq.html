Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is FutureFinds?

FutureFinds is your premier destination for the latest gadgets and technology products. We offer a wide range of electronics, including smartphones, laptops, accessories, and more, with options to buy, pre-order, or rent.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website. Fill in your details including username, email, and password. Once registered, you can start shopping and tracking your orders.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within our local region. Please check our shipping information page for specific countries and regions we serve. International shipping options may be available for select products.

Ordering & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), mobile payment services like bKash, and secure online payment through SSLCommerz. Cash on delivery is also available for select areas.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also log into your account and visit the "Orders" section to view your order status and tracking details.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order if it's still in "Pending" status. Once the order is processed and shipped, cancellation may not be possible. Visit your order details page to cancel an eligible order.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping options are available for faster delivery. Delivery times may vary based on your location and product availability.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Same-day delivery is available in select metropolitan areas for in-stock items ordered before 2 PM. Additional fees may apply for same-day delivery service.

What if I'm not available for delivery?

If you're not available, our delivery partner will attempt delivery again the next business day. You can also contact us to reschedule delivery or arrange pickup from a nearby location.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Items must be in resellable condition. Some products like software and personalized items may not be eligible for returns.

How do I return an item?

Log into your account, go to your order details, and click "Return Item." Follow the instructions to print a return label and package your item. We'll process your refund once we receive the returned item.

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days after we receive and inspect your returned item. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.

Pre-Orders & Flash Sales

How do pre-orders work?

Pre-orders allow you to reserve upcoming products before they're officially released. You'll be charged when the item ships, and you'll receive priority delivery once the product becomes available.

What are flash sales?

Flash sales are limited-time offers with significant discounts on select products. These sales have limited quantities and are available on a first-come, first-served basis.

Can I cancel a pre-order?

Yes, you can cancel pre-orders before they ship. Once the product is ready and ships, normal return policies apply. Visit your pre-order section in your account to manage your pre-orders.

Rental Services

How does the rental service work?

Our rental service allows you to rent high-end gadgets for a specified period. You pay a daily rate, and the total cost depends on the rental duration. Perfect for trying out new technology or short-term needs.

What happens if I damage a rented item?

Normal wear and tear is expected. However, significant damage may result in additional charges. We recommend handling rented items with care and reporting any issues immediately.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can extend your rental period if the item is available. Contact our customer service team or manage your rental through your account dashboard.

Still Have Questions?

If you couldn't find the answer to your question, our customer support team is here to help!
